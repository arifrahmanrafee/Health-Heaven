Dr.Devi Prasad Shetty

Dr.Devi Prasad Shetty is the best cardiac surgeon in the world with more than 36 years of experience. He has performed over 120,000 heart operations, of which 40,000 have been performed on children. He started Narayana Health (then known as Narayana Hrudayalaya) in Bangalore. Narayana Health has now become one of the largest healthcare groups in India providing world-class, affordable, safe, and quality care for all.

Rosemary Gladstar

Rosemary Gladstar, known as the godmother of modern herbalism, she has captivated a global audience with her wealth of knowledge and passion for herbal healing. With over 50 years in the field, she's not only penned eleven influential books, including favorites like Herbal Healing for Women and Herbal Recipes for Vibrant Health but also founded the International Herb Symposium and United Plant Savers.

Dr. Sunil Prakash

Dr. Sunil Prakash is a highly accomplished and respected kidney specialist in Delhi, India. He holds an impressive educational background, having completed his MBBS and MD in Internal Medicine from prestigious medical institutions. Dr. Prakash further specialized in Nephrology and obtained a DM degree from AIIMS, Delhi, making him an expert in the field of kidney-related diseases and disorders.
