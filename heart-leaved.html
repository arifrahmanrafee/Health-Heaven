Giloy

Scientic name : Tinospora cordifolia

T. cordifolia (family: Menispermaceae), locally known as ‘pipolti’, is traditionally used as a treatment for gastric trouble and ulcers. Moreover, the anti-ulcer efficiency of T. cordifolia extracts was assessed in ethanol and the pylorus ligation-induced ulcer model whereas a remarkable reduction of ulcer index, gastric volume, and total acidity sup-ported the efficacy of the extract as an anti-ulcer agent. The major phytocomponents which were isolated from the T. cordifolia, sesquiterpene tinocordifolin, tinocordifolioside, sesquiterpene glycoside, arabinogalactan, tinocordiside, makisterone, magnoflorine, and palmatine could play a vital role in reducing the illness.

Health Benefits

  • Tinospora cordifolia is known for its immunomodulatory properties. It helps enhance the body's defense mechanisms against infections by boosting the activity of white blood cells and improving overall immune function.
  • Tinospora cordifolia is often used to reduce fever. It is especially effective in treating chronic fever and febrile conditions associated with malaria and dengue.
  • The plant aids in detoxification and supports liver function. It helps protect the liver from damage caused by toxins and enhances the regeneration of liver cells.
  • Giloy(synonyms) promotes healthy digestion by improving gut health and reducing digestive disorders like indigestion, constipation, and hyperacidity.
  • Tinospora cordifolia can help regulate blood sugar levels, making it beneficial for people with diabetes. It enhances insulin secretion and sensitivity.
  • Giloy has adaptogenic properties, which help the body cope with stress and anxiety. It can improve cognitive function and overall mental health.

Disease Prevention

  • The antimicrobial properties of Tinospora cordifolia help prevent and treat various infections, including bacterial, viral, and fungal infections.
  • Its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties can help prevent chronic diseases such as heart disease, cancer, and neurodegenerative disorders.
  • Regular use of Giloy can help manage diabetes by regulating blood sugar levels and improving insulin sensitivity, thereby preventing complications associated with diabetes.
  • The anti-inflammatory properties of Giloy can help prevent and manage arthritis and other joint-related issues by reducing pain and inflammation.
