Bottle Gourd

Scientic name : Lagenaria siceraria

L. siceraria of the Cucurbitaceae family is commonly known as ‘bottle gourd’. The fruits, leaves, roots, and seeds of L. siceraria contain different active constituents such as vitamin B, amino acids, and ascorbic acid (vitamin C). The leaves are cooked and taken by women as a potherb and to relieve the pain during menstruation. The syrups from the fruits are also used to treat bronchial abnormalities like pectoral cough, asthma, etc. The extract of L. siceraria has both strong anti-ulcer and antioxidant activities, though the molecular mechanisms of both anti-ulcer and anti-oxidant activities were not investigated. However, the doses of 250 and 400 mg/kg were safe as there was no indications of signs of toxicity or mortality. From another study, a dose up to 1000 mg/kg could be safe after the repeated administration of methanolic extract of L. siceraria fruit for 28 days.

Health Benefits

  • Bottle gourd has a high water content, making it an excellent vegetable for hydration. It helps maintain fluid balance in the body, especially during hot weather.
  • This vegetable is low in calories and fat, making it ideal for weight management and low-calorie diets. It can be included in meals to provide volume and satiety without adding extra calories.
  • The fiber content in bottle gourd aids in digestion and helps prevent constipation. It supports a healthy digestive system by promoting regular bowel movements and relieving gastrointestinal issues.
  • The potassium and magnesium in bottle gourd help regulate blood pressure and support cardiovascular health. Its low sodium content also contributes to heart health by maintaining healthy blood pressure levels.
  • Bottle gourd acts as a natural diuretic, helping to flush out toxins from the body through increased urine production. This detoxifying effect supports kidney function and overall detoxification processes.
  • Due to its low-calorie content and high fiber and water content, bottle gourd can aid in weight management by promoting a feeling of fullness and reducing overall calorie intake.
  • Bottle gourd has a cooling effect on the body, making it beneficial during hot weather to prevent heat stroke and dehydration. It helps regulate body temperature and provides relief from heat.
  • Bottle gourd supports liver health by aiding in detoxification and reducing the burden on the liver. It helps maintain liver function and promotes overall liver health.

Disease Prevention

  • Bottle gourd is low in carbohydrates and has a low glycemic index, making it suitable for people with diabetes. It helps regulate blood sugar levels and can be a part of a diabetic-friendly diet.
  • The combination of fiber, potassium, and magnesium in bottle gourd helps lower cholesterol levels and maintain healthy blood pressure, reducing the risk of heart disease.
  • By aiding in weight management, bottle gourd helps prevent obesity-related conditions such as type 2 diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, and metabolic syndrome.
